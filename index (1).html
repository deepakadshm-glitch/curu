FAQ

Good to know before you join.

Is this registered investment advice? +

No. NiftyEdge shares educational market commentary and personal trade ideas for informational purposes. We are not a SEBI-registered investment advisor or research analyst. Always do your own research and consider your own risk tolerance before trading.

How many calls should I expect? +

The free channel typically carries 6–8 calls a week, plus a daily wrap-up. VIP members get the same calls earlier, along with additional context and direct access to ask questions.

Do you show past performance? +

Yes — a monthly recap is posted in the channel showing every call made that month, including the ones that hit stop-loss, not just the winners.

Can I cancel VIP anytime? +

Yes, VIP is billed monthly with no lock-in. You can leave the group at any time and simply won't be renewed for the next cycle.
